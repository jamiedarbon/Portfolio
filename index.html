Jamie Darbon is a Game Developer living in Brighton, England.

About Me

I am a self-motivated and driven individual who is always striving to improve and willing to learn. From a young age, I have spent time learning and developing skills which I now use to
bring myself an income as a self-employed artist and designer online. During my time in secondary school, I balanced my education with making comics which I published every
week, helping me to develop time management skills. I’ve always had an interest in computers and video games, which have fueled my professional development and informed
my career choices. Along with developing artistic skills, I spent a lot of time growing up developing technical skills relating to game development and am proficient in various
coding languages such as C, C++, C#, HLSL, Java and Javascript.

In my spare time, I like to work on a range of creative projects such as video editing, art, and game development. I am currently using my skills to work on an experimental 3D game about
graffiti in the Unity engine where I have created all the assets myself, including 3D models and shaders written in HLSL. I am also semi-fluent in French, having studied it up to A-Level
and continuing to study it in my spare time in the years since.
